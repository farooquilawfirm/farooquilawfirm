About The Practice

Honest & Solution-Based Approach

Farooqui Law Firm is a leading legal practice located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. With years of experience, the Firm offers top-tier legal services in Civil, Corporate, Waqf, Criminal, and Family law.

We practice in all Courts across Maharashtra, including the Bombay High Court and all its Benches. Farooqui Law Firm is proud to be respected and trusted by clients for its dedication and experience in handling sensitive court matters.

Our lawyers are available to vigorously protect your interests, not only in an adversarial family court setting but also in mediation & counselling, and through mutual consent Courts. We follow an honest and solution-based approach while keeping the confidentiality of every matter.
